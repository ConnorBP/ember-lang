# ember v0.6 benchmark results

ember (native JIT, tree-walking stack-spilling codegen) vs AngelScript (bytecode interpreter).
Hot-path: compile once, then N iterations timed. ember/AS ratio < 1 = ember faster.

| workload | ember ms/iter | AS ms/iter | ember/AS | verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| fib(32) | 21.66 | 157.08 | 0.14 | ember >>AS (native wins big) |
| tight_loop (1e8) | 182.03 | 1072.60 | 0.17 | ember >>AS (native wins big) |
| nested_calls (1e7) | 179.21 | 1129.04 | 0.16 | ember >>AS (native wins big) |
| mandelbrot (200x200, iters=50) | 4.38 | 8.43 | 0.52 | ember faster |
